Slow Cooked Beans

Mmmm you smell it

Its beans slow cooked. slowly being cooked. cooked so slowly. Waiting gradually as it calmly soaks under a heated fire. Cautiously emiting a smell luring you towards it and away from it at the same time. This is beans.

Ingredients

  • 4 cans (15-1/2 ounces each) great northern beans, rinsed and drained
  • 4 medium carrots, finely chopped (about 2 cups)
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 cups fresh baby spinach, coarsely chopped
  • 1 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, patted dry and chopped
  • 1/3 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • 1/3 cup minced fresh parsley
  1. In a 3-qt. slow cooker, combine the first 7 ingredients. Cook, covered, on low 4-5 hours or until carrots are tender, adding spinach and tomatoes during the last 10 minutes of cooking. Stir in cilantro and parsley.
